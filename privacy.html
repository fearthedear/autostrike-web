Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoStrike Golf ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the AutoStrike Golf iOS and watchOS app and our website at autostrikegolf.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address when you sign up or request early access.
  • Round data: golf scores, GPS-derived shot locations, courses played, club selection, and stats you enter.
  • Location data: precise GPS location while a round is active, used for distances, shot detection, and aerial maps. Location is only collected during active rounds.
  • Device information: iOS / watchOS version, device model, app version — used for crash reports and support.
  • Support submissions: messages, email, and any context you choose to send us via the in-app or website support form.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide GPS distances, automatic shot tracking, scoring, and handicap calculation.
  • Sync your rounds, bag, and handicap across your iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Show you live multiplayer leaderboards when you join a group round.
  • Respond to your support requests and improve the app.
  • Send you an early-access invite if you signed up for one.

3. Data Storage

Your account, round data, and support submissions are stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS in Singapore). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Sharing

  • We share your name and live score with players in the same multiplayer group when you choose to join one.
  • We use Apple-provided services (Sign in with Apple, App Store) which have their own privacy policies.
  • We do not run third-party advertising networks inside the app.

5. Your Rights

You can request export or deletion of your account and round data at any time by emailing us through the support page. Account deletion is also available from inside the app.

6. Children

AutoStrike Golf is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified inside the app.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us via the support form.
